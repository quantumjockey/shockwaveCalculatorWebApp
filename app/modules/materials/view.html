This software takes in user data from high pressure and temperature shockwave experiments and calculates shock properties based on modeling implemented by Thomas J. Ahrens in his paper:

Shock Wave Techniques for Geophysics and Planetary Physics

Thomas J. Ahrens

Seismological Laboratory - California Institute of Technology - Pasadena California 91125

Published in 'Methods of Experimental Physics' - Vol 24, Part A, Page 185.

Copyright 1987 by Academic Press, Inc.

Full text can be viewed at: http://www.fas.harvard.edu/~planets/sstewart/ahrens/index.html (Item#: 179 - Parts 1 and 2)

All data for default materials was taken from the table:

Shock Wave Data For Minerals

Thomas J. Ahrens, Mary L. Johnson

Seismological Laboratory - California Institute of Technology - Pasadena California 91125

Published in Mineral Physics & Crystallography: A Handbook of Physical Constants - Pages 144-171
